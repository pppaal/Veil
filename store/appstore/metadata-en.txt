[Description]
VEIL is a messenger designed with privacy as the first principle.

Core principles
• No phone number. No email. No real name required.
• Every message is end-to-end encrypted on-device. Servers never see plaintext.
• No recovery path. If you lose the device, the conversations go with it. This is the VEIL safety model.

Cryptography
• X25519 key exchange + AES-256-GCM symmetric encryption
• Ed25519 device signatures
• HKDF-SHA256 session key derivation

Features
• 1:1 and group conversations with encrypted attachments
• Typing indicators, presence, optional read receipts
• Local app lock (PIN, biometrics)
• Encrypted device transfer
• Korean and English UI

Privacy posture
• No ads. No tracking. No third-party analytics.
• Designed to meet Korea's PIPA requirements.

Safety note
VEIL deliberately has no account recovery. If your device is lost, conversations cannot be restored. This is by design.

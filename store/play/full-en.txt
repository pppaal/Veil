An end-to-end encrypted messenger with no phone number and no account.
VEIL is a private, secure chat app where even the server can't read your
messages. Instead of finding friends by phone number, you connect with a
single handle — anonymous, encrypted, and ad-free.

■ Why VEIL
• Sign up with no phone number, no email, no real name — an anonymous secure messenger.
• Every message is end-to-end encrypted on-device. Servers never see plaintext.
• No ads, no tracking, no third-party analytics.

■ Core principles
• No phone number. No email. No real name required.
• Every message is end-to-end encrypted on-device. Servers never see plaintext.
• No recovery path. If you lose the device, the conversations go with it. This is the VEIL safety model.

■ Cryptography (real encrypted chat)
• X25519 key exchange + AES-256-GCM symmetric encryption
• Ed25519 device signatures
• HKDF-SHA256 session key derivation
• Payloads are sealed just before transit and never stored server-side.

■ Features
• 1:1 and group conversations with encrypted attachments
• Typing indicators, presence, optional read receipts
• Local app lock (PIN, biometrics)
• Encrypted device transfer
• Korean and English UI

■ Who it's for
• Anyone who needs a private messenger without exposing a phone number
• Journalists, activists, and professionals who need a secure messenger for sensitive conversations
• People who want a clean encrypted messenger with no ads and no data collection

■ Privacy posture
• No ads. No tracking. No third-party analytics.
• Minimal data collection. The in-app consent screen lists every field.
• Designed to meet Korea's PIPA requirements.
• Open source — the code is public and auditable.

■ Safety note
VEIL deliberately has no account recovery. If your device is lost, conversations cannot be restored. This is by design. For multi-device access, use the built-in encrypted device transfer.
